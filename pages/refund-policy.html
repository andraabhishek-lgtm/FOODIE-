Our Refund Policy

We want every order to be perfect. Here's how we handle refunds when something goes wrong.

Last updated: June 18, 2026

✅ Eligible Refund Scenarios

You are eligible for a full or partial refund in the following cases:

  • Order was never delivered and marked as delivered incorrectly
  • Wrong items delivered (not what you ordered)
  • Food arrived in a spoiled, damaged, or inedible condition
  • Duplicate charges on your payment method
  • Order cancelled by the restaurant before preparation
  • Technical errors resulting in double payment

⏱️ Refund Timeline

Payment MethodRefund Timeline
UPI / Wallet (FoodieExpress Credits)Instant – 2 hours
Debit / Credit Card3 – 5 business days
Net Banking5 – 7 business days
FoodieExpress WalletInstant
Cash on Delivery (COD)Credited as FoodieExpress wallet balance

❌ Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refund request raised more than 24 hours after delivery
  • Order consumed partially and then reported as unsatisfactory
  • Cancellation requested after the restaurant has begun preparation
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer
  • Promotional or free items included in the order

🔄 How to Request a Refund

  1. Open the FoodieExpress app or website and go to My Orders
  2. Select the affected order and tap Report an Issue
  3. Choose the issue type and provide a brief description
  4. Upload photos if required (for wrong/damaged items)
  5. Our support team will review and respond within 24 hours

💡 Tip: Refunds for UPI and wallet payments are processed the fastest. If you want quicker resolution, choose these payment methods.

📞 Contact Support

If you haven't received your refund within the stated timeline or need urgent help, reach out to our 24/7 support team.
