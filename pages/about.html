?? Our Journey

From a Small Idea to a Big Movement

FoodieExpress was born during the 2020 lockdowns when two food-loving friends noticed that great restaurants were struggling to reach their customers. They built a platform that connected local gems to hungry people nearby.

What started as a small operation in Bangalore quickly grew to cover 25+ cities across India. Today, we partner with 500+ restaurants and serve 50,000+ happy customers every month.

Our mission remains the same � deliver happiness, one meal at a time, while supporting local food businesses and creating jobs in every community we serve.
